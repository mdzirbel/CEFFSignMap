The goal of the game is to be the last player with balls.

Click to select a ball, then click where you want to go.

The farther away you click from the ball, the faster it will go.

Balls which fall off of the green field will disappear.

Balls which run into opponent's converters will turn into their team.

Refresh to return to the main menu from anywhere.
