The goal is to survive as long as possible.

Use the left and right arrow keys to go to the left and right.

Use the up and down keys to use the powerups.

Up makes you invincible for two seconds. The red bar indicates it is ready to be used.

Down stops the cars while you are holding it. The green bar depeletes while you are holding it and refils when you are not holding it

Hit enter to restart quickly.
